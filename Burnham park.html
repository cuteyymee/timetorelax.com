Welcome to Burnham Park

Introduction

Burnham Park, officially known as the Burnham Park Reservation, is a historic urban park located in downtown Baguio, Philippines.

It features a man-made lake known as Burnham Lagoon, lawns, wooded areas, paved pathways and numerous flower beds.

History

Burnham Park was originally designed by a renowned American architect and urban planner, Daniel Hudson Burnham, in 1904. He as well designed the original plan for Baguio City itself. Mister Burnham was a renowned architect and urban planner from Chicago.
